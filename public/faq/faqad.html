What is an ad and how can I create it?

An ad is the entity composed of different settings you can select, such as geo targeting, operating system, creative, etc, that will be displayed to your targeted audience. You can organize your ads in Campaigns. You can create a new ad by clicking on Create an ad in the dashboard, or in My ads/ Create an ad.

Where will my ads be displayed?

Your ads will be displayed in Shortfundly app, according to the definitions of placement, targeting and schedule you have selected.

How are my ads priced?

You pay for your ads in CPM (cost per mille) impression. It means that we will charge you a fixed price per 1,000 times the ad is displayed to your target audience.

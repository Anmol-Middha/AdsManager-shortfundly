WHAT ARE TARGETING OPTIONS?

Targeting options are settings you apply to your ads to narrow down the reach of your ads to a specific audience.

WHAT TARGETING OPTIONS CAN I HAVE FOR MY ADS?

Targeting options are available according to the ad products available to you in the Create an ad section. Geo targeting, gender and operating system are examples of targeting options that can be available during ad creation.

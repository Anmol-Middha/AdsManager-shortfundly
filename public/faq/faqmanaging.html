HOW CAN CANCEL OR PAUSE AN AD?

You cannot cancel a purchased ad, but you can pause an ad. When you do so, the ad will stop serving impressions. Truecaller can, however, cancel or pause your ads at anytime, according to the Terms and Conditions. If that happens, you will be notified via email.

HOW CAN I EDIT AN AD?

For purchased ads, you can only edit the following targeting options: geo targeting, frequency capping and time targeting. For ads part of campaigns that are in the cart, you can edit all selected settings in the Ad Details page. You cannot edit finished ads.

ONE OF MY ADS STOPPED SERVING IMPRESSIONS, WHAT SHOULD I DO?

You can check if your ad or creatives have been canceled or paused in the Ad Details Page. You can also contact Truecaller by submitting your message in the Contact form. The form is represented by the question mark at the bottom of every page.

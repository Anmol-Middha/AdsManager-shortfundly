WHAT TYPES OF CREATIVES CAN I SUBMIT?

You can submit: - Image files You can submit image file in the formats jpeg, jpg, png and gif. You can also add a tracking script to your image files. - Third party tags Third party tags/Third party scripts are snippets of HTML5 code that contains an image. They are provided by third party vendors or partners which generate browser calls to external third party servers. With a Third party tag you can verify and track your ads. - Native ads Native ads are ad formats which have predefined components that you can use to create your ad. In fields that are predefined in the Self-service Platform, you can add your image, logo and headline, to create your ad. This allows for a more flexible and easy way to design your ads.

HOW CAN I SUBMIT NEW CREATIVES?

You can submit creatives(s) during the ad creation flow; by editing ads you have in the cart or by accessing My ads/ Ad Details Page for booked ads.

CAN I EDIT A CREATIVE FOR A RUNNING AD?

No, you cannot edit a creative for a running (active) ad, but you can pause/resume a creative or upload a new creative.

HOW CAN I SUBMIT A NEW CREATIVE FOR A RUNNING AD?

You can submit a new creative to an existing ad, both running (active) and inactive, in My ads/ Ad Details Page. However, you cannot submit new creatives for finished ads.

CAN I EDIT A CREATIVE IN THE CART?

Yes, you can edit and upload new creatives for ads that are in the cart and have not been purchased yet.
